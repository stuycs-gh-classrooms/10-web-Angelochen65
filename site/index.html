Can you feel your heart beating in your chest? Sort of? Put your hand on it. It is more apparent? It works very hard every day to make sure that you're alive so here's a heart appreication/cardiovascular anatomy website.

The cardiovascular system is responsible for sending blood throughout your body to ensure that your organs are functioning. Red blood cells carry a protein called hemoglobin which binds to oxygen. This oxygen is necessary for cells to end aerobic cellular respiration.
